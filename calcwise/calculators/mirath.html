The Quranic Basis of Mirath

Allah says in the Quran: "Allah commands you regarding your children: for the male, what is equal to the share of two females." (Surah An-Nisa 4:11). Islamic inheritance law is one of the most precisely defined areas of fiqh, with fixed fractional shares (Faraid) assigned to each heir category.

Order of Priority (Simplified)

  • Spouse — receives fixed share (1/4 or 1/8 for wife; 1/2 or 1/4 for husband)
  • Children — sons get double the share of daughters (Asabat)
  • Parents — each gets 1/6 if deceased has children
  • Siblings — inherit only if no children or father
  • Extended family — complex rules; consult scholar
