Islamic Investment Structures

  • Murabaha — cost-plus sale; bank buys asset and sells at disclosed profit
  • Sukuk — asset-backed Islamic bonds; return tied to real asset performance
  • Musharakah — joint venture; partners share profit and loss proportionally
  • Mudarabah — silent partnership; investor provides capital, manager provides work
  • Ijara — leasing; return comes from rental income on real assets
