Sunnah Fasting Days

  • Monday & Thursday — The Prophet ﷺ fasted regularly on these days
  • Three White Days — 13th, 14th, 15th of each Islamic month
  • Day of Arafah (9 Dhul Hijjah) — Expiates sins of two years
  • Ashura (10 Muharram) — Expiates sins of the previous year
  • Six days of Shawwal — Combined with Ramadan equals a full year of fasting
