💡 The Prophet ﷺ said: "Sadaqah does not decrease wealth." (Muslim). Give consistently, even if small.

Types of Sadaqah

  • Sadaqah Jariyah — ongoing charity (building a masjid, funding education, planting a tree)
  • Sadaqah Nafilah — voluntary general charity given at any time
  • Sadaqah al-Fitr — obligatory at end of Ramadan (separate from voluntary)
  • Smiling — the Prophet ﷺ said even a smile is Sadaqah
