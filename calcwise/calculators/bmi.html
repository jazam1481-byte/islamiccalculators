Islamic Guidance on Health

Islam encourages moderation in all things, including eating. The Quran says: "Eat and drink, but be not excessive." (7:31). The Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ included regular physical activity, fasting (which has proven health benefits), and avoiding excess food. Maintaining good health is considered part of fulfilling one's Amanah (trust) over the body Allah gave us.
